Grenville Sweet Potato Pudding

This recipe isn't so different from many of the sweet potato puddings or casseroles made today. Instead of sugar to add a little sweetness, it uses sherry (the original recipe called for sweet wine from Spain).

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Coconut Milk
  • Brown Sugar
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Raisins
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the sweet potatoes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine grated sweet potatoes, coconut milk, brown sugar, and melted butter.
  3. Add all-purpose flour, raisins, vanilla extract, cinnamon, nutmeg, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  4. Preheat the oven to the specified temperature.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until the pudding is set and the top is golden brown.
  7. Allow the pudding to cool before serving.
  8. Serve and enjoy your Grenville Sweet Potato Pudding!
